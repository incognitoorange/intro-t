messi

Lionel Messi is a football player from Argentina who plays for Inter Miami. He has won the Ballon D'Or, the annual award given to the best player in the world, 8 times, 2022 FIFA World Cup winner and an Olympic gold medal winner in 2008. He was born in 1987 in Rosario, Argentina's third-biggest city.

kylian mbappe

Without a doubt, Kylian Mbappé is a footballer who makes the world sit up and take notice. Leading his team to victory in the FIFA World Cup at the age of just 19 and getting one step away from a second before even turning 24, Mbappé has already put his name next to the best ones that ever played the sport.
