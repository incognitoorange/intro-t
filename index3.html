neymar jr

Neymar made his professional debut on 7 March 2009, despite being only 17 years old. He was brought on for the last thirty minutes, in a 2–1 win against Oeste.[10] The following week he scored his first goal for Santos against Mogi Mirim. One month later, on 11 April, Neymar scored the decisive goal in a 2–1 win against Palmeiras in the 2009 Campeonato Paulista semi-final first leg.[18] In the final, however, Santos suffered a 4–2 aggregate defeat to Corinthians.[19] In his first season, Neymar racked up 14 goals in 48 games.[10]
